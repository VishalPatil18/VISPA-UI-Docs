Typography

VISPA-UI includes an expertly-crafted Typography which has a total of 5 amazing google font-families which can make your UI building experience effortless.
VISPA-UI provides you seven themed colors and six variants each of ten beautiful colors.

Font Families of VISPA-UI

Poppins

Poppins is the default font-family of VISPA-UI. To use Poppins in your project just add the class of poppins to the container.

This is a Poppins text 

              
              
<p class="poppins">This is a Poppins text</p>
